About

Computer Science degree holder and Self taught developer. For the last 3 years I've taught myself web development daily. Along the path of learning, I have acquired freelance jobs under my brand CourDevelops. I'm a dedicated self starter with front-end web developer skills, plus a little experience on the Backend. My natural knack for customer service made me a user centric developer. I want to develop simplistic, productivity apps that are easy to use and make workdays and workflow more efficient for people.


Education: Bachelors of Science degree in Information Systems and Cybersecurity from ITT Technological Institute
